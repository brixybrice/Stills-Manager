Open Folder

Help — Stills Manager

Opening a folder

Click Open Folder to browse your filesystem and open any folder containing images (JPG, PNG, TIF).

The open folder is stored in the URL — reloading the page automatically reopens the same folder.

Gallery

Click a thumbnail to add or remove the image from the active selection.

Cmd+click or the expand button on a thumbnail to open the image fullscreen.

Selected images show a teal border and a numbered badge indicating their position in the current selection.

Selections

Selections are organised as tabs at the top of the right panel. Each tab holds an ordered list of images.

+ creates a new tab · Double-click a tab name to rename it · the remove button deletes the tab.

Images within a selection can be reordered by drag and drop.

Selections are saved automatically to .stills-selections.json in the open folder (debounced at 350 ms).

Fullscreen (Lightbox)

A vertical carousel on the left shows surrounding images — click any thumbnail to jump to it.

The context badge (top right) shows whether you are browsing the Gallery or a named selection. The #N badge shows the image's position in the active selection.

The Up and Down buttons (and their keyboard shortcuts) move the image within the selection order.

PDF export

The PDF button in the selection panel exports one image per page in 16:9 landscape format.

If a watermark is configured, it is composited over every page.

Paramètres

The Paramètres button opens a two-tab settings panel.

Watermark — configure text, font, size, opacity and angle, with a live preview on a synthetic image. The watermark is applied to all PDF exports when text is set.

Contact Sheet — configure grid layout, aspect ratio, gap, export resolution and watermark. Live canvas preview. Export as JPG, PNG or PDF. The Append to PDF export checkbox adds the contact sheet as a final page to the main PDF.
